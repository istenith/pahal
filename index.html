What is Pahal?

Team ISTE here at NIT Hamirpur is taking an initiative PAHAL to help freshers in the admission process by setting up a help-desk, in-person guidance, and in every way possible. 😀

This website is to provide necessary tools and information.

This website is mobile-oriented to minimise load on users' devices. Dark mode is recommended for power savings but light mode is also available and fully fuctional.
