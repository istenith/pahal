Below is the list of documents required for admission through JOSAA
1
Provisional JoSAA Seat Allotment Letter 2019
⏷
Issued by JoSAA on seat acceptance.
2
Passport size photographs
⏷
Three passport size photographs similar to one uploaded in JEE Main 2019 application form
3
Candidate Undertaking
⏷
Download from official JoSAA 2019 portal
4
JEE Main Score Card 2019
⏷
Download from the official results of JEE Main
5
JEE Main Admit Card 2019
⏷
Download and get a hard copy of it
6
Class 10th Marksheet
⏷
Class 10th Standard Mark Sheet or Date of Birth Proof
7
Class 12th Marksheet
⏷
Class 12 (or equivalent) Mark Sheet and Pass Certificate
8
Valid photo Identity Card
⏷
Aadhar card is preferred
9
Medical Certificate
⏷
Get it from any government medical center or from the college dispensary
10
Category Certificate
⏷
If applicable
11
PwD Certificate
⏷
If applicable
12
OCI Certificate or PIO Card
⏷
If applicable
13
Registration and Filled Choices for Seat Allotment
⏷
Download from official JoSAA 2019 portal
